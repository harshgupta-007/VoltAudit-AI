# 🎨 VoltAudit AI Brand Style Guidelines

This document details the visual identity, typography system, color palettes, and assets mapping rules.

---

## 🎨 Color Palette
- **Primary slate (`#0F172A`):** Dark slate representing base system containers, terminal background layers, and cards background.
- **Accent blue (`#3B82F6`):** Vibrant blue highlighting active AI Workforce specialist progress tracks and success states.
- **Alert amber (`#F59E0B`):** Amber gold signifying compliance score holds, math discrepancies, and manual review triggers.

---

## 🔤 Typography System
- **Main Family:** **Outfit** (loaded via Google Fonts).
- **Headings:** Outfit Bold (weight `700` or `800`) for landing slides and metrics.
- **Body & Logs:** Outfit Regular (weight `400`) and Outfit Medium (weight `500`) for descriptions, captions, and tables.
