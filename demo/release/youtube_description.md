# 🎥 YouTube Video Description: VoltAudit AI Launch

Welcome to the official launch presentation of **VoltAudit AI** - an enterprise-grade utility bill auditing platform powered by Google's Agent Development Kit (ADK) and Model Context Protocol (MCP).

In this walkthrough, we present how VoltAudit AI coordinates a workforce of specialized AI agents to analyze complex utility invoices, audit contract rates, calculate peak hours multipliers, and protect against payment leaks due to double-billing historical duplicate records.
