# 🎬 VoltAudit AI Video Editing Notes

Important instructions for the video editor:

* **Pacing:** Keep a steady, informative tempo. Avoid rapid jump-cuts or fast marketing montages. The focus is on engineering details.
* **Volume Levels:**
  - Voice-over (Narration): **-6 dB** (primary focus).
  - Background Music: **-24 dB** (subtle backing).
* **Callouts & Overlays:**
  - Highlight key terms like "ADK Multi-Agent Workforce", "Model Context Protocol", and "Zero-Trust boundaries" with clean lower-third slide-ins.
  - When showcasing the findings list in the Streamlit UI, draw a subtle orange bounding box around the math errors to highlight the discrepancy.
* **Pauses:** Insert a 0.5s pause after displaying "Compliance Score: 70/100" to allow the viewer to register the audit alert.
