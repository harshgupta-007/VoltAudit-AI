# Engineering Task 004A — Define the Enterprise AI Workforce

## Role

You are a Distinguished AI Systems Architect at Google responsible for designing enterprise AI organizations composed of specialized AI workers.

You are continuing the development of VoltAudit AI.

The project has completed repository initialization, enterprise architecture, and enterprise capability modeling.

This task must extend those approved artifacts.

Do not redesign previously approved architecture.

Do not implement application code.

---

# Repository Intelligence Phase (Mandatory)

Before making any changes:

1. Review all existing project specifications.
2. Review the Enterprise Architecture.
3. Review the Enterprise Capability Model.
4. Review all Architecture Decision Records (ADRs).
5. Review the Project Constitution.
6. Review the Global Context.
7. Identify every approved business capability.
8. Identify capability ownership.
9. Summarize your understanding.
10. List every artifact that will be created or modified.

Do not proceed until repository understanding is complete.

---

# Objective

Design the complete Enterprise AI Workforce for VoltAudit AI.

This workforce represents the organizational structure of the platform.

Workers represent business specialists.

Workers do not represent software classes or implementation objects.

The workforce becomes the organizational blueprint for future ADK agents.

---

# Business Context

VoltAudit AI assists finance and audit teams in validating electricity generation invoices.

The workforce should mirror how a real enterprise audit department would divide responsibilities among specialists.

Favor specialization.

Avoid general-purpose workers.

---

# Workforce Design Principles

The AI Workforce must:

* align with approved business capabilities
* have clear ownership boundaries
* minimize overlap
* maximize explainability
* promote collaboration
* support scalability
* support future extension
* remain implementation independent

Do not design ADK agents.

Design enterprise workers.

---

# Workforce Organization

Design the organizational hierarchy.

Define:

* Executive coordination
* Specialist workers
* Supporting workers
* Human interaction points

Describe reporting relationships where appropriate.

Produce organization diagrams.

---
